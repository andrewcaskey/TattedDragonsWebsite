About 

Welcome to Tatted Dragons,  the underground web3 community where your NFT doubles as your membership to the Tatted Dragons Tatsu. All Dragons come with a proof of ownership stored on the Ethereum blockchain.   

Each Tatted Dragon  represents a base level of membership and each NFT consists of varying and unique levels of community access and perks. Each Dragon is unique and no two are exactly alike.
